Honey Garlic Salmon

Description

A tasty recipe that is full of both vital nutrients and a powerful taste. This recipe is easy to make and is sure to make a big impact for your next dinner night.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Fry Salmon
  2. Add Garlic
  3. Add Soy Sauce
  4. Add Honey
  5. Serve on Rice
