Chocolate Protein Smoothie

Description

A high impact, high protein smoothie. This recipe is sure to give you the nutrition you need to hit your protein goals.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate protein powder
  • Banana
  • Strawberry
  • Oat milk
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Add banana
  2. Add strawberries
  3. Add protein powder
  4. Add oat milk
  5. Add peanut butter
  6. Blend, serve, and enjoy!
